Answers to Frequently Asked Questions

Which payment method are supported?

Currently we support credit card as well as PayPal payments.

What is my support used for?

Your support goes toward keeping OWAD running on a day to day basis. It also enables new features to become part of the OWAD experience.

How long is the support for?

For as long as you want. We will book the amount every month until you decide to cancel.

How do I change my level of support?

In order to change your level of subscription simply cancel the current subscription here and subsequently choose a new level of support here

Do I receive an invoice for my support?

You will automatically be sent an invoice every month for as long as you support OWAD. If you need adjustments to your invoice, please contact support@owad.de
